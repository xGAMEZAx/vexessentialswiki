WHAT IS VEX ESSENTIALS?


It's an add-on (script) for setting up a server/realm in less than a day, It include a custom GUI and a bunch of cool plugins. The pack Need to have API BETA Experimental feature checked and it works on xbox, ps4, w10 and pocket edition.
